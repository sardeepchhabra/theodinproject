Butter Paneer

Description:

        Paneer Butter masala is a North Indian delicacy that translates to ”cubes of 
        Indian cottage cheese cooked in a rich and creamy onion-tomato-cashew sauce.”

Ingredients:

  • Paneer
  • Butter
  • Tomato Paste
  • Kasoori Methi
  • Spices
  • Cooking Oil
  • Cream

Steps:

  1. Heat the oil in a pan on medium heat. Once hot add onion, ginger, garlic and cashew nuts.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and mix.
  3. Cook until tomatoes are soft and mushy.
  4. Now add turmeric powder, red chili powder and coriander powder.
  5. Stir well and cook for a minute. Turn off the stove and let the mixture cool down slightly.
  6. Now add that mixture into the blender jar along with a splash of water.
  7. Wipe out the same pan using a paper towel and melt the butter in it on medium heat.
  8. Add whole spices (cloves, cinnamon, cardamoms).
  9. Within 30 seconds add the prepared puree.
  10. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  11. Now add water and mix well. Simmer for 4-5 minutes.
  12. Add paneer, garam masala, crushed kasoori methi and cardamom powder.
  13. Add heavy cream, mix and cook for a minute.
  14. Your restaurant style paneer butter masala is ready.
